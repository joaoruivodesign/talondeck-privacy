# TalonDeck Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 18, 2026_

TalonDeck ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal media backlog tracker for
movies, TV shows, books, and games. This policy explains what data the app
handles. We designed TalonDeck to keep your data on your device.

## Data we collect

**We do not collect, sell, or share personal information.** TalonDeck has no
user accounts, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking SDKs.

## Data stored on your device

The cards you create (titles, suits, progress, notes, and similar) are stored
locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. This data never leaves your
device except through Apple's own iCloud device backup if you have that enabled,
which is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Information sent when you search

When you search to add a card, your search text is sent to our request proxy and
forwarded to the relevant catalog provider so we can return matching results and
cover art:

- **The Movie Database (TMDB)** for movies and TV shows - https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy
- **IGDB / Twitch** for games - https://www.twitch.tv/p/legal/privacy-notice/
- **Open Library** for books - https://openlibrary.org/privacy

These requests contain only your search query and are not linked to your
identity. Our proxy exists solely to keep API credentials off your device and
does not store personal information. Cover images are loaded directly from these
providers' public image servers.

## Subscriptions

TalonDeck Pro is an auto-renewable subscription handled entirely by Apple. We do
not receive or store your payment information. Purchases are governed by
[Apple's terms](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/dev/stdeula/).

## Children's privacy

TalonDeck is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
personal information from them.

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised
